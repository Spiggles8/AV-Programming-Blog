Welcome

Welcome to the AVProgramming.Tech site. This website is designed for those interested in learning more about the Audio Video programming world. My name is Sean and I have been an AV Programmer for the last 5 years working for an Integrator and now for a state college. I am by no means an expert in the field, however am more than willing to share what I have learned so far. Please enjoy taking a look around!
